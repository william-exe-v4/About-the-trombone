The Trombone

The trombone is a simple but interesting instrument. It's the only instrument in our band to feature no buttons

*except the spit valve
inventor uncertain time ~15th century
material brass pitch range C2 - F5+

The creation

The trombone has existed for a very long time. Although the inventor is uncertain, it is sure that the trombone dates back to the Renaissance era. The instrument is made of brass with the mouthpiece being silver-plated, though plastic trombones and non-conductive mouthpieces do exist.

From its origins...

The trombone originated from the trumpet (the oldest one with no valves).
